About the project

EVsTV is a small project made by a team of university students at Claude Bernard Lyon 1 University for the course Data Visualization. Its purpose is to present a visualization with d3.js that builds awareness regarding impacts and pollutions produced during the life-cycle of electric vehicles compared to those of ICEV (internal combustion engine vehicles).
